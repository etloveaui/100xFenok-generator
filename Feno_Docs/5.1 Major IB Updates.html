Bank of America led today's analyst activity with multiple significant moves, including downgrades of Li Auto to Neutral from Buy with a $26 price target (down from $31) following weak Q2 results and reduced volume guidance 4. The firm also upgraded International Paper to Buy from Neutral, raising the price target to $57 from $55 based on expectations of higher pricing and operating rates exceeding 95% in 2026 20.

JPMorgan made notable upgrades, raising Futu Holdings' price target to $270 from $200 while maintaining Overweight following strong Q2 results and significant growth in clients and assets under management 10. The firm also resumed coverage of Siemens with an Overweight rating and EUR 300 price target, placing it on Positive Catalyst Watch ahead of its capital markets day 24.

Citi executed several downgrades, including Aspen Insurance Holdings to Neutral from Buy with a $37.50 price target (down from $43) reflecting Sompo's acquisition offer 3. The firm also downgraded Cooper Companies to Neutral from Buy, cutting the price target to $72 from $97 8. However, Citi raised CBRE's price target to $190 from $160 and increased 2025 adjusted EPS estimates to $6.18 from $5.93 2.
