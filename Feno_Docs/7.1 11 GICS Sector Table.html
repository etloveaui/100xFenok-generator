Key Sector Rotation Themes

Industrials momentum strengthened on manufacturing PMI rebounding to 53.3, the highest since May 2022, with sharp increases in new orders and hiring. XLI attracted $340.2M inflows on August 21st amid policy tailwinds from infrastructure plans and manufacturing incentives.3
